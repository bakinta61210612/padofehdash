Where Does Adin Ross Live and What is His Net Worth in 2023?

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Adin Ross currently lives in Los Angeles, California, and he has a net worth that is estimated at around $16 million. The 23 year-old makes his fortune from his enviable career as a social media star and gamer, something he has been doing since 2019.

Interest in Adin’s wealth came about as a result of a report in 2023 that claimed he had a deal worth $150 million with Kick, which would require him to stream exclusively on the platform. Although it has since come to light that there is no truth to the claim, there are still many more things about Ross and his fortune.

Adin Ross Now Lives in California

Although he currently lives in California, it was in Boca Raton, Florida, that he was born on October 11, 2000. It was also here that his parents of Jewish ethnicity mostly raised him alongside his sister, Naomi. From there, he moved to New York City, where he lived for a while.

He did not stay here for a long time before he moved again, but this time around, to Three Rivers, California. While he was in California, he attended the Woodlake Union High School. Since then, he has continued to live in California to date.

How did Adin Ross Get Famous?

Adin’s fame is tied to his career as a gamer. He started gaming together with his older sister, Naomi Ross. Soon, he played the NBA 2K game on Twitch and later became known for NBA 2K20 together with Bronny James. His fame continued to grow until Lebron James placed a call to him while he was streaming.

He continued to build and sustain his fame through gaming and hosting e-date streams. By 2021, his following on Twitch had already grown to over a million. At the time, his streams were pulling in over 30,000 views.

While he continued to build his following on Twitch, he was also doing the same on Instagram and YouTube, as well as other online platforms. He was also associated with other online personalities such as Andrew Tate, Logan Paul, and IShowSpeed.

Ross managed to get his name in the news by getting into several controversies, which got him banned on Twitch a number of times. At a point.
