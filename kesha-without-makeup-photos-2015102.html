Kesha Goes Without Makeup, Dons Sweats While Smiling: Photo

August 2024 · 1 minute read

Fresh-faced—and beautiful!

Better than ever on the road to recovery, a smiling Kesha stepped out in Los Angeles on Saturday, Feb. 7, appearing calm and happy without a stitch of makeup on her face.

The "Warrior" songstress, 27, kept it casual in a semi-sheer long-sleeved top, drawstring sweats, and UGG boots. She tied her signature blonde locks up into a loose bun, confidently flaunting her makeup-free face.

Taking it slow since entering herself into rehab for an eating disorder in January 2014, Kesha has kept a pretty low profile as of late. However, she did spend the end of last month in Brazil for a series of four concert performances. (And she must have enjoyed herself, as she Instagrammed, "Brazil rules!" three weeks ago.)

The "TiK ToK" singer, who now goes by "Kesha Rose," has also been spreading good vibes via her Twitter page. On Feb. 6, she tweeted, "Be kind, my animals," next to a quote photo that read, "People often forget that kindness is free."
