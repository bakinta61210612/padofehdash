Maggie Elizabeth McGraw is Tim McGraw's 2nd Daughter

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Maggie Elizabeth McGraw is the 25-year-old daughter of Tim McGraw, the American country musician. She is the second daughter of the musician and her mother, Faith, who is also a famous musician.

Maggie is the second of three children of her parents, and she remains the least known member of her family since she decided to maintain a very private life away from the entertainment business, unlike her parents and two sisters. As a result, her fame is mostly tied to the popularity of her parents, who are among the most successful country singers in the United States.

Maggie Elizabeth McGraw was Born in 1998

Maggie Elizabeth McGraw was born on August 12, 1998, which makes her 25 years old. She was birthed to her famous parents, Tim McGraw, and Faith Hill, in the United States, where she was also brought up alongside her two siblings.

She is of caucasian ethnicity with a diverse heritage, as her father is of Scottish, German, Irish, Dutch, Italian, and French descent, while her mother is of Cornish, Dutch, English, French, German, and Scottish descent.

Her earlier educational pursuits remain unclear, but it has been reported that Maggie McGraw attended Stanford University, from where she graduated in June 2020.

Her Parents Are Famous Musicians

As indicated, Maggie’s parents are Tim McGraw and Faith Hill, both of who are very famous individuals. Her father was born Samuel Timothy McGraw on 1 May 1967, making him now 56 years old.

A singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor, Tim began his professional career in 1990, although it was in 1993 that he released his first album. More than three decades later, the country singer has close to 20 albums to his name and has sold millions of copies of his works. He has also won many awards, including three Grammy Awards, Academy of Country Music, Billboard Awards, and American Music Awards.

He has also appeared in various movies and TV shows, including the 2014 film that earned him an MTV Movie Award nomination, Friday Light, and The Shack (2017).

Faith Hill is also a big name. She was born Audrey Faith Perry on September 21, 1967. The 56-year-old is also a country singer and an actress who started her career in 1993. She has a rich discography and has also won many awards, including five Grammy Awards, Country Music Association Awards, and American Music Awards.

Despite their successes, Maggie’s parents have still maintained a great family life. The couple has been married since 1996. While it was the first marriage for Tim, it was not for his wife. Before they met, Faith was previously married to music publishing executive Daniel Hill. The marriage was sustained from 1988 to 1994, when they divorced.

Maggie Elizabeth is a Climate Sustainability Expert

With parents who are musicians, an older sister who is an actress, and a younger sister who is a model, one would think Maggie would find herself a place in the entertainment business, but that is not the case. In 2016, Faith Hill shared a video of her daughter singing Taylor Swift’s Bad Blood. With that, many came to believe that she was going to follow the musical path, but that was not to be.

According to Tim McGraw, his daughter has always had an interest in the ocean and marine biology, and she is a free diver. This was what led her to study climate control while in college. She returned to the college after graduation and obtained a Master’s in Sustainability Science and Practice.

While that may be her interest, The Hill reported that as of 2022, Maggie Elizabeth McGraw was working with a Democratic representative from Tennessee, Congressman Jim Cooper as his staff assistant and legislative correspondent.

That said, she came very close to becoming a professional musician when she was in college. At the time, she formed a musical group known as Sister Supply and was its lead singer. The band had some shows, but she did not continue with that path after her education.

Meet Maggie Elizabeth McGraw’s Siblings

Maggie is not the only child of her parents; she has two siblings. While it is clear that Tim and Faith have recorded many achievements in their musical career, what may even be a greater achievement for the duo is their three kids. Meet Maggie’s two siblings, Gracie McGraw, and Audrey McGraw:

Gracie McGraw

Gracie is the oldest child of the family. She was born a year after her parents got married on May 5, 1997, which means she is now 26 years old. Described by her father as a strong and independent woman, Gracie is not just the oldest of her father’s children, but she is the best known. She is a graduate of New York University and has since begun a career in Hollywood as an actress.

Thus far, she has appeared in several films and TV shows, including Tyler Perry‘s If Loving You was Wrong, in which she played Tanya. While she might have made her name as an actress, she has also picked up some music skills from her parents. Although she doesn’t seem to be interested in pursuing it professionally, the actress has shared some clips of herself singing with a powerful voice on her social media channels.

As regards Maggie’s relationship with Gracie, the two sisters are very close, although Maggie is more of a private individual.

Audrey Caroline McGraw

The youngest member of the family is Audrey Caroline McGraw, who happens to be quite more famous than Maggie. She was born on December 6, 2001, and is currently 22 years old. She got her education from Ensworth Red Gables Campus and graduated from high school in 2020.

Audrey doesn’t seem to be interested in pursuing the career paths of her parents in becoming a musician neither does she seem interested in becoming an actress like her older sister. Instead, she is working on a modeling career.

The 5 feet 8 inches model has already been a part of several important photoshoots and has modeled for different brands, including Tory Burch. Just like her sister, even though she is not into music, she has also shown that she has some musical side by sharing some of her videos while singing.

Needless to say, she has a very good relationship with both her sisters, Maggie and Gracie.
