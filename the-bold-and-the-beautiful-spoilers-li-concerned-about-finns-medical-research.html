Li Concerned About Finn's Medical Research

September 2024 · 2 minute read

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers suggest that Li Finnegan (Naomi Matsuda) is concerned about Dr. John “Finn” Finnegan’s (Tanner Novlan) medical research.

He is trying a new experimental treatment on Eric Forrester (John McCook), which could bring him back to life. However, there are concerns about possible symptoms and side effects. Keep on reading to learn more.

B&B Spoilers: Li Worries About Finn’s Medical Research

According to the Bold and the Beautiful spoilers from Daytime Confidential, Li becomes concerned with Finn’s medical research. This means she might learn about the new experimental treatment that he’s been using to save Eric’s life.

Some fans wonder if this treatment will even work. This was brought up on the SoapCentral.com Message Boards.

One B&B fan asked: “What’s Li worried about now? He’s trying to help Eric… Guess he doesn’t tell her.”

Another user responded: “I agree that Finn must be keeping his medical research a secret from Li.”

A third user noted: “Li seems like a very controlling person. Finn not having her in the loop will drive her crazy. She will but I’m sure.”

According to the Bold and the Beautiful spoilers, Finn kept his medical research a secret from Li. He didn’t want to concern her about the situation involving Eric.

Also, Eric doesn’t want everyone to know that he’s dying. However, most of the Logans and the Forresters know what’s going on with him.

The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: Li Turns On Finn

The spoilers could also suggest that Li will eventually turn on Finn. She might be upset when she learns he’s been playing around with this new treatment option behind her back.

She could argue that Finn should’ve come to her first. On the flip side, Li could be concerned that Finn is researching DNA.

She might become paranoid because Li and her sister Poppy (Romy Park) are hiding something from him. There are still questions among B&B fans as to why Li doesn’t like her sister Li.

She teased that Poppy was a wild and carefree woman who had an affair with an established doctor. However, those details haven’t come to light as of yet.
