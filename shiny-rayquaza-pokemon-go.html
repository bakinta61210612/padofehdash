How To Catch Shiny Rayquaza In Pokemon Go &amp; How To Find It First!

September 2024 · 3 minute read

In Pokemon Go, Shiny Rayquaza will be back during Weather Week. Players may use this guide to learn where to find and how to catch this legendary Pokemon. Currently, a weeklong event called Weather Week is centred on Pokemon with a weather-related theme. The special Shiny Rain Castform, which is the first time this form has ever been Shiny in any Pokemon game, is one of the major novelties to come from this event. Before the event ends, players will want to capture this rare Pokemon. However, Rayquaza, who will once more appear in raid battles, is another reward that players can discover. Players can use this guide to learn where to look in Pokemon Go to find a Shiny Rayquaza.

Although Shiny Rayquaza has been in the game for a while, users have never been able to find their own because Raid Battles are the only way to get it. Rayquaza will start making raid appearances once more during Weather Week beginning on Saturday, March 27, 2021, at 10:00 a.m., and lasting until Sunday, March 28, 2021, at 8:00 p.m. local time. Players have less than two days to attempt to catch this Pokemon in its Shiny form. Here are a few methods gamers might prepare for your Pokemon Go Shiny Rayquaza search.

How To Get Shiny Rayquaza in Pokemon Go

Only from Saturday, March 27, 2021 from 10:00 a.m. until Sunday, March 28, 2021 at 8:00 p.m. local time will Shiny Rayquaza appear in Raid Battles. Make sure to stock up on Remote Raid Passes before this occurs (players can only carry 3 at a time). Join a raid when one is available, and then wait until at least 4 additional players have joined. Any less will be difficult to overcome. Players won’t be able to tell if it’s a Shiny until the capture phase, which is where the trick lies. A Shiny Rayquaza will always be encountered by at least 1 person in a lobby with 20 players. Just try to get by with the players you have since COVID-19 makes it a little difficult to locate a full lobby. Just keep taking part in Rayquaza raids until the Shiny shows up. You won’t find it unless you look for it, but it is not assured to show up.

One of the most attractive Shiny Pokemon is Shiny Rayquaza. In addition to being quite powerful, it also looks nice in your collection. Wishing you luck on your Shiny Hunting expedition.
