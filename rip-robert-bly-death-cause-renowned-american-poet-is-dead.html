The media is full of condolences on Robert Bly’s death. The poet Robert Bly was 95 years of age as of now and due to celebrate his birthday next month. 

I am hearing that poet Robert Bly may have passed today.

A few years ago, when he was 88 years of age, he was detected to have Alzheimer’s disease. There is news of him passing away on Sunday, November 2021.

He was the winner of the 1968 National Book Award for poetry. Everyone love the legendary and admired his work. His passing news was revealed by Loren Niemi through Facebook.

His obituary has not been released yet publicly so, we do not know how he passed away. His Wikipedia page has not been updated yet on his death. 

Without having many assumptions on his death, it would be better to wait to hear from authorized publications on it. May his soul rests in peach, and his family may get strength.

the passing of the legend writer is indeed a great loss to the poetic sector. Though he is not around us, his books, poems, and work will always keep him alive.
