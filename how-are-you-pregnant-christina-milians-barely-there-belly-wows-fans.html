How Are You Pregnant?: Christina Milians Barely There Belly Wows Fans

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Christina Milian is proving that just because you’re pregnant doesn’t mean you have to cover up. And fans are amazed that what she’s revealing shows little more than a small baby bump.

The singer and former reality TV star on Saturday shared a couple of snapshots as she posed on a balcony in Cannes, France. She wore a crop top with a matching pair of high-waisted pants with high heels along with a pair of oversized sunglasses and hoop earrings. Her hair was styled in a topknot to complete the look.

“Stealing Kisses from the Sun in Cannes, France.. 🤗” read Milian’s photo set caption that she posted to Instagram Nov. 9.

The 38-year-old second-time mom-to-be had been in the famed city to support her French singer-songwriter boyfriend M. Pokora, also known as Matt Pokora. Pokora was honored at the NRJ Music Awards this past weekend.

But why the “Dip It Low” singer was in town didn’t matter to fans. They readily focused instead on the star’s belly, which some say is remarkably small for the singer to be in her second trimester.

“Pregnant where???? I Stan 🙌🏾 ❤️”

“How are you pregnant ? 😩😩 I’m not and my stomach is bigger than yours 😭”

“Hold up. Where the baby😂”

“I guess you get to choose the days you wanna be pregnant 😂😂😍😍😍”

“@christinamilian you are so naturally beautiful and you wear that pregnancy so well! You’re so hot!! 💋💋”

Milian’s younger sister, Liz Milian, also commented saying, “Dude you look amazing.”

Her actress sister appreciated the compliment, noting that despite her small size she still feels large sometimes.

“@lizmilian_ awe lizzy! Thank you! Means a lot you know I feel like a house at time.. lol but then I’ll take a pic and I’m like.. oh.. well it’s not that bad I guess. Just feel extra ‘filled’ lol.”

The singer and her beau of two years are expecting their first child together next year. The baby, a boy, will join Milian’s 9-year-old daughter, Violet, whom she shares with music producer/songwriter/singer The-Dream.

After the couple learned they’d be welcoming a son, Milian disclosed to Us Weekly in September that they’re having a hard time settling on a name.

“We’re thinking about names, but we still haven’t come across anything that we love,” she said. “We’re, like, definitely back and forth on that one because French accents and English accents don’t sound the same on names. I didn’t care about that, but that’s his thing. I guess in French some things don’t sound as good. So that’s the only thing that gets in the way of finding a name.”

Milian added that she’s ready to throw in the towel and simply “disagree” on whatever name Pokora comes up with until they can settle on one.
