Bantu knots: 4 Celebrities who have rocked the fabulous hairstyle

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Minnie Dlamini was recently criticised on social media after she described her Bantu knots hairstyle as "ghetto". The hairstyle is far from that and has been used by black women as a protective style for years. It recently gained popularity among celebrities as they have begun to embrace rocking natural hair.

We takes a look at a few times celebs looked fierce with Bantu knots.

PAY ATTENTION: Click “See First” under the “Following” tab to see We News on your News Feed!

1. Beyoncé sports Bantu knots on her Already music video.

The music icon recently debuted her movie, Black Is King, which pays homage to black people and Africa. It only made sense for the starlet to rock Bantu knots in one of the music videos in the hit film. She looked gorgeous with a braided version of the popular style.

2. Boity Thulo looks gorgeous with the hairstyle

Our very own Boity Thulo has also rocked the fabulous hair-do. The media personality is known to try out different hairstyles but has recently gone for a more natural look with protective styles like cornrows and Bantu knots.

3. Natasha Thahane slays in natural knots

Actress Natasha Thahane also showed off her stunning beauty while wearing the protective hairstyle. She looked nothing short of amazing.

4. Ciara set the internet on fire with her hairstyle

When Ciara debuted her Bantu knots, social media users went berserk. Sis looked incredible and it was a refreshing change from her long weaves.
