Five Tips To Get Your Hands On Mcdonald's New Disney100 Happy Meal Toys

August 2024 · 4 minute read

McDonald’s has released new Disney100 Happy Meal Toys as part of the celebration of the Walt Disney Company’s 100th anniversary. These toys feature 62 Disney character figurines, which come in packs of two in each Happy Meal. The toys are officially supposed to be available from October 31 through December 4, but they have already started to arrive at some McDonald’s locations.

For Disney fans, collecting these Happy Meal toys is a must. However, with the toys being in high demand and only available for a limited time, it can be challenging to get your hands on them. To help you out, we have compiled a list of five tips to increase your chances of snagging these Disney100 Happy Meal Toys. Whether you’re a collector or just a fan, these tips will help you get your hands on the toys before they’re gone.

Understanding Disney100 Happy Meal Toys

Disney100 Happy Meal Toys are a limited edition collection of 62 Disney character figurines that come in packs of two in each Happy Meal. These toys are now available at McDonald’s reportedly through December 4th (or while supplies last).

Why They Are Unique

The Disney100 Happy Meal Toys are unique because they celebrate the 100th anniversary of The Walt Disney Company. The collection features popular Disney characters from classic movies like “Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs” to recent releases like “Frozen 2.” Each toy is intricately designed and crafted to resemble the character it represents.

The Connection with McDonald’s

Disney and McDonald’s have a long-standing partnership that dates back to the 1980s. McDonald’s has been a sponsor of various Disney events and attractions, and the two companies have collaborated on many promotions over the years. The Disney100 Happy Meal Toys are just the latest example of this partnership.

To get your hands on these exclusive toys, there are a few tips to keep in mind. Check with your local McDonald’s to see if they have the toys in stock. Some fans have suggested that the toys come in waves, so each location will get random boxes of them and have to sell all in the boxes before the next ones come. Be sure to act fast, as the toys are only available for a limited time.

Five Tips to Get Your Hands on the Toys

McDonald’s new Disney100 Happy Meal Toys are all the rage, but they can be difficult to get your hands on. Here are five tips to help you secure these new toys:

Utilize Online Platforms

One of the best ways to get your hands on the new Happy Meal Toys is to utilize online platforms. There are several websites and social media platforms where collectors and fans share information about the toys. For example, TikTok creator and Disney fan @Discupcakes shares the number of each box and the corresponding character on her TikTok page [1]. Utilize these platforms to stay informed and increase your chances of getting the toys.

Be Informed About Release Dates

Another important tip is to be informed about release dates. McDonald’s releases the new Happy Meal Toys in waves, and it’s important to know when each wave will be released [1]. Keep an eye on McDonald’s social media pages and website to stay informed about release dates.

Leverage Toy Swapping Communities

If you’re having trouble finding a specific toy, consider leveraging toy swapping communities. These communities are made up of collectors and fans who are willing to trade toys with each other. You can find these communities on social media platforms like Facebook and Reddit. Join these groups and post what you’re looking for to increase your chances of finding the toys you want.

Remember to be respectful and fair when trading with others.

By utilizing online platforms, being informed about release dates, and leveraging toy swapping communities, you can increase your chances of getting your hands on the new McDonald’s Disney100 Happy Meal Toys.
