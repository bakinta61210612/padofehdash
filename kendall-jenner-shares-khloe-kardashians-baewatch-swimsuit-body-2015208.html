Sister sister! Kendall Jenner and Khloe Kardashian showed off some major (sun-kissed!) skin while lounging on a boat in St. Barts on Wednesday, Aug. 19. The pair, who are currently on a family vacation with the rest of the Kardashian-Jenner clan, shared their sisterly love in a series of fun photos on Instagram.

“Baeeeewatch,” Jenner captioned one shot, in which she and Kardashian are kneeling together at the bow of the boat. In the photo, the 19-year-old model wears a slimming black one-piece suit and has two hands raised up in peace signs.

Kardashian, meanwhile, looks unprepared for the photo as she glances over at Jenner, though her outfit is on point: a red one-piece Baywatch-inspired swimsuit and a black baseball cap with the word “Compton” emblazoned across the front.
