Perrie Edwards Stuns at Teen Choice Awards Amid Zayn Malik Diss

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Zayn who? Perrie Edwards is taking the high road when it comes to her split with former One Direction star Zayn Malik — stunning on the stage at the 2015 Teen Choice Awards show while her ex stoops to belittling her on Twitter.

On Saturday, Aug. 15, Malik, who called off his engagement to Little Mix singer Edwards via text message earlier this month, retweeted a post which indicated he preferred Fifth Harmony's single "Worth It" to Little Mix's "Black Magic."

"Which single is better?" Shady Music Facts asked via Twitter, followed by the instructions to retweet if you liked Fifth Harmony's song, or favorite if you preferred the Little Mix single.

Malik's heartless retweet received tons of fan reaction, and led to the #ZaynHasNoChill hashtag trending on Twitter. But rather than get embroiled in an online battle, Edwards instead decided to have her say on stage — looking slim and sexy in a stunning feathered miniskirt and matching white crop top at the star-studded Los Angeles awards ceremony.

Not only did Edwards look amazing as she and her bandmates took to the stage, she also won an award for being the Choice Music Breakout Artist of 2015, along with fellow singers Jesy Nelson, Leigh-Anne Pinnock, and Jade Thirlwall.

A source told Us previously that the high-profile music couple had split after Malik requested time out to think about their relationship. "They had previously argued and talked about their relationship, and he said he needed time away to think," the source revealed. "Then he never went back to her to talk more. He finished it with a long-ass message. She tried to call him after but he wouldn’t pick up."
