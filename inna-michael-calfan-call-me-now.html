It's A Hit! Inna Joins Forces With Michael Calfan For Catchy &quot;Call Me Now&quot;

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Don’t call us now, we are busy dancing to Inna’s new smash hit!

The days are getting colder, the nights are getting longer and the overall-mood just gets a little gloomier. Luckily for us, Michael Calfan and Inna have just delivered the perfect antidote for that!

The two artists have just released their first collaboration ever, titled “Call Me Now”, and it is even greater than we could have imagined. It all starts off with a dreamy guitar intro that quickly merges into the first chorus. A chill electro-beat sets in that is then being paired with INNA’s dreamy vocals. “Don’t call me now /’Cause I’m better off without you / I don’t need you around / Don’t need no one to hold me down”, she sings during the upbeat chorus that will make you want to get up and dance and sing along.

“Call Me Now” is a catchy masterpiece that ends too soon and will make you want to repeat it over and over again.

The song arrives as part of a very busy year for both artists: French DJ Michael Calfan dropped hits with superstars such as Martin Solveig (“No Lie”) while Romanian superstar INNA has gifted her fans lots of tracks this year that saw her return to her electro-pop past. Standouts included “Nobody” or “Not My Baby”. The fact that this latest release can top those hits makes it so much more special.
