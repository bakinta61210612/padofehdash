Gina Rodriguez sends Golden Globe Dress to a fan

If you didn’t love Gina Rodriguez before, you will after finding out that she actually gave her fan the dress she wore at the Golden Globe Awards 2015 so that she can wear it at her prom! After requesting Gina’s dress on Twitter, a fan (Jessica Casanova) got a reply from Jane The Virgin star, who requested her location in order to send the dress.

”Would love to wear your Golden Globe dress to prom” tweeted Jessica (@TheJessica_C).

”Where do you live? I only own my first Golden Globe dress from last year… Maybe we can make this happen” replied actress Gina Rodriguez (HereIsGina).

This began an exchange of tweets about how excited Jessica was when she received the dress – I don’t think she expected a response from her, never mind the actual dress!

Could we love Gina any more right now? She got her ‘rise to fame’ when she was cast the part of Jane in Jane The Virgin, an American TV show comedy drama about a 20-something-year-old virgin who gets accidentally inseminated and becomes pregnant. After two series’ of the show, Gina has become more well-known and is currently working on a movie with Mark Wahlberg, Dylan O’Brien and Kate Hudson.

As much as she loves her acting career, she loves her fans even more! It’s difficult to come across celebrities who appreciate and love their fans, but Gina is one of the sweetest people you could possibly meet. It’s difficult to get a like or a mention from your favourite celebrity – but a dress?! Gina is once again proving she slays at everything – even life.
