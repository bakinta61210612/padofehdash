Nota Baloyi Bashes Zahara After News of Her Being Hospitalised Went Viral

August 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Music executive Nota Baloyi bashed multi-award-winning singer Zahara after she got hospitalised
  • Nota shared a tweet where he said he thanked God for getting her medical care after years of supposed alcohol abuse
  • Some social media users agree with Nota while others think he was being insensitive

Bathong, Nota Baloyi has no chill whatsoever. The controversial music executive had something to say about the award-winning artist Zahara.

Nota Baloyi bashes Zahara

The controversial Baloyi has been making headlines for the past years, and recently he trended after he called out and bashed Zahara after she got hospitalised with a liver condition.

Nota retweeted a post on his Twitter page that spoke about Zahara being admitted to hospital after falling critically ill and he bashed the singer, saying he thanks God that He got her hospitalised after years of alcohol abuse. The music executive wrote:

"Thank God, this will put an end to the years of alcohol abuse she’s been through, sadly at the cost of her liver… Imagine drinking so much your liver fails before you’re even 40 years old? Young girls today have no role models, it’s tragic!"

When contacted for a comment, Nota Baloyi told Briefly News that he hopes Zahara will stop slandering DJ Sbu. He said:

"I hope she recovers from alcohol addiction and stops slandering DJ Sbu… I can say what I want, I don’t abuse people or substances!"
